Intro Research Team CV

I am an Assistant Professor at the Pennsylvania State University Department of Astronomy and Astrophysics. My group applies new joint analysis algorithms and machine learning methods to the next generation of wide-field surveys for cosmology and time-domain science applications. We are interested in multi-wavelength studies of variable and state-changing AGN, tidal disruption events, and strongly lensed supernovae. Find out more about our current research here.
